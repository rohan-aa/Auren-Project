THE CRAFT

Where tradition meets innovation — every piece tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship

Artisanal Excellence

For over two decades, AUREN has been synonymous with uncompromising quality. Each garment is born from a dialogue between master artisans and modern design, resulting in pieces that transcend seasons.

Our atelier in Florence combines century-old techniques with contemporary innovation, ensuring every stitch meets our exacting standards.

Our Values

Sustainable

Ethical Craftsmanship

Made in Italy

The Process

01

Design & Development

Each collection begins with sketches and fabric research. Our design team travels the world seeking inspiration, from the architecture of Milan to the landscapes of Tuscany.

02

Pattern Making

Master pattern makers translate designs into precise templates, considering every curve and contour for the perfect fit.

03

Cutting & Assembly

Expert cutters handle the precious fabrics with care, followed by skilled seamstresses who bring each piece to life.

04

Finishing & Quality Control

Every garment undergoes rigorous inspection — buttons, seams, and finishes are checked to ensure perfection.
